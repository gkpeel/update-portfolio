About Me

Forbes Magazine calls Fishtown, Philadelphia the “Hottest New Neighborhood” - and while I agree that it's a great place, I just call it home.

As a former freelance Producer for video content, a Project Manager at a boutique digital marketing agency, and an Executive Assistant at a production company, I enjoy collaborating with others to make quality content.

The traditional developer annecdote of learning to code during my teenage years doesn't apply in this case. No, sadly I began learning how to code at the ripe old age of 24. My ongoing love for coding and development was born from a mix of thriftiness, a bootstrapping attitude, and the need to create a website for a film that I was working on.

In the years since, my love for development has risen in stride with my skill-level. As an ongoing learner of new technologies, design theories, and marketing best-practices, I bring knowledge that allow new businesses to immediately establish credibility with their visitors.
